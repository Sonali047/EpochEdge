About Our Project EpochEdge

Fullstack Developer!

Suppose you attend an event and click photos in camera of a friend or photographer. That friend or photographer will upload all photos to "Upload your image" section of EpochEdge website. You just need to open the EpochEdge website and go to "Get all session image" section to download all images or "Get your image" section to download only those images you are present.

Important Note: Photographer will upload the photos with a specific username called Session username and you must know that username to collect the images.

How to get session image

This section helps you to get all the images of the session or event you are attend.

Click on SignUp/SignIn → Features → Get all session image →Click on Let's go → Enter the username of the Session → Your images will start to download automatically.

close

How to upload your images

This section helps you to upload all the images of the event that the first step to reach the images to each attendee.

Click on SignUp/SignIn → Features → Upload your images → Click on Let's go → Enter the Session username → Choose the images and upload → View uploaded images → Back to the portal.

close

How to get your own images

This section helps to get only those images you are present. It'd just need to scan your face.

Click on SignUp/SignIn → Features → Get your images → Click on Let's go → Enter the Session username → Click on Next → Take a snap → Your images will start to download automatically.
